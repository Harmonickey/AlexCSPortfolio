How we came to be:

We rose out of a friendship of our founders. Early in the 10th grade, they enjoyed a Computer Programming class together. Without realizing it, our founders
became the closest of friends. a few months later, they decided to make games, apps, and utilities under a common name. We grew out of this.


How our name came to be:

Originally, Saurutobi came up with a simple name: JAM. Since all three founders wanted their names represented, and all the initials were there, they decided
vertical lines to make the seperation clear. Therefore, J|A|Mstudios came to be. But, too many people and companies were associated with the word JAM; there
were no .coms free for jamstudios, either. The original website was hosted for free, because highschoolers are poor, under jamstudios.000webhost.com. on
December 3rd, Saurutobi decided to re-name, to be something new. He spent hours searching around, brainstorming. Finally, after nothing insane sprung from
his normally fruitfull mind, he asked the most creative person he knew. She took only 20 minutes, and returned with 'lazerbearswithkeyboards.org'. Seeing this
Saurutobi was insanely happy, but the name was long. One simple abbreviation later, and LbKstudios.net and to be. J|A|Mstudios still exists, but as a pet
project of the founders.


Who is Saurutobi?:

The Director of LbKstudios. He takes the ideas he's presented with, refines them, and puts them into action. Most recently, he got annoyed with some code that
he had swapped, and had to copy and paste, a few times to swap the code back. He became annoyed with this, and decided to start a "Paste-Switcher". More info
can be found under the 'Ideas' tab. Saurutobi is the main person in charge of everything. he does the graphic design, the code, the ideas. He can do it all
but the specialists in their respective areas, Harmonickey-programming, Turtlegod0-graphics, take what he did and delve deeper, enriching it.

Saurutobi is a complicated man. He was born in Germany, but moved to America in 1998. He has become the leader through hard work, and persistence. He has
become known for his intelligence and strength of mind. Being that as it is, he isn't the most likeable person in the work. His personality speaks far louder
than he can, and this has proven complicated, as he values personal strength and opinions, and those that do not voice themselves to him often, and rightly,
feel they are being disrespected and ignored. Very rarely does Saurutobi ask for someone's opinion, unless that someone is Harmonickey or Turtlegod0. He
prefers to be told what people think, which very often angers others because they don't like feeling ignored. His unique personality shapes who he is, and who
he surrounds himself with intelligent and talented people. Because of his strange tastes, he comes off as a complicated character most people except those of
the strongest willpower dislike.

Saurutobi is a man with a music sense that would seem strange to anyone except his closest companions. He likes Showtek, Wildstylez, Headhunters, Psy,
Hardstyle, Brostep, Dance, and selective K-pop. He loves dancing, listening to music, watching movies, and generally enjoying life and the music. A few years
ago, he decided to start a techno-music podcast called Technoweek. Since then, Technoweek has been aborted for fear of DMCA violations and those repercussions.


Who is Harmonickey?:

Harmonickey is Saurutobi's counterpart. Calm, collected, this man knows his programming.


Who is Turtlegod0?:

Turtlegod0 brings up the trifecta. He is always calm and quiet, very respectfull of everybody. He is the go-to guy for graphics, having created the LbKstudios
logo and homepage graphics. While Saurutobi attacks all his graphic problems with MSPaint and Fireworks, Turtlegod0 uses Adobe's suite of graphic tools, to
