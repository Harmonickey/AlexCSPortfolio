A MediaPhile's Blog DMCA Compliance Requirements


To submit a valid DMCA Notice, you must follow the following guidelines:

  1. Send your Claim to abuse@amediaphilesblog.com.
  2. The Claim must be written in the English Language, it must be understandable and grammatically correct and in polite form.
  3. The Claim must be sent from a company email address. Claims sent from free service emails(AOL, Yahoo, Gmail, etc.) will be immediately rejected.
  4. Identify yourself as an owner of the copyrighted work or exclusive rights that you believe were infringed, or you are a person acting on behalf of such owner, and provide a physical signature (when it is in paper form) or electronic signature (when it is in electronic form) as evidence of such.
  5. Identify the copywrited work that you believe is being infringed upon.
  6. Provide a Web URL on amediaphilesblog.com that contains the material.
  7. Present evidence that shows that the content is legally copyrighted by you or the owner of such material whom you represent.
  8. State which articles of the DMCA you feel are being violated.
  9. State your contact information, including name, street address, phone number, and valid email address. If you are acting on behalf of the copyright owner please also describe your affiliation with the copyright holder (e.g attorney, vendor, agent).
  10. State that the information in the notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is believed infringed.
  11. If you feel multiple works are being violated or on multiple webpages, you must submit a seperate DMCA Notice for each in full.
  12. Any messages, including Takedown Notices are accepted as plain text. Any enclosed attachments will not be opened or examined due to security reasons and the Notice will be returned immediately as incorrect. Attachments shall be processed only if sent on demand of amediaphilesblog.com.

All information above must be included, lack of any required materials or incorrect presentation of such will result in the immediate return of of the Notice.


Upon reciept of the Notice, a return email will be sent notifying you of the reciept of the Notice.


Depending on the situation, you will be notified of the progress of the DMCA proceedings. If amediaphilesblog.com feels your DMCA rights are not being violated in the manner you stated, you will be notified. Further communication will be necessary.


If you feel your rights have been violated, please do not hesitate to submit a notice, provided it be submitted following the above criteria.
